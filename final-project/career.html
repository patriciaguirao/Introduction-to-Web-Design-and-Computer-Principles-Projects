School

Grade School: Singapore American School

From Kindergarten to 12th grade, I attended Singapore American School (an American international school in Singapore).

While this school is primarily made for American expats, due to my dad initially being considered an expat (as he came to Singapore from the Philippines), I attended the majority of my grade schooling here.

My school follows the AP system in high school (I took AP Computer Science, AP English Language, AP Psychology, AP Calculus AB/BC, AP Chemistry, and AP Economics). Furthermore, my school created further advanced courses which they called "Advanced Topics" courses (I took AT Computational Physics and AT Computer Science: Data Structures). Furthermore, I was very involved in dance, Peer Support, various service clubs, Prom Committee, Forensics, TEDxYouth, and various honor societies.

I graduated with a 4.3 GPA (Magna Cum Laude), 1530 SAT, 5s on almost every AP Exam (except AP English Language where I got a 4), 800 Math Level 2 SAT II, and 790 Chemistry SAT II

University: New York University



I currently attend New York University and am in the College of Arts and Science. I study Computer Science and Data Science.

While I will graduate in May 2024, I will only be a part-time student in Spring 2024.
