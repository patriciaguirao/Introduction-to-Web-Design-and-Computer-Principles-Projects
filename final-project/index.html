About Me

Hi! My name is Patricia Marie Guirao and I'm a 22-year-old Filipina who was born and raised in Singapore but is currently based in New York City (I also lived in Thailand for 2 years when I was a toddler). While I'm Filipino by citizenship and lived in Singapore the majority of my life, I went to an international American school growing up. While my main cultural influences are Filipino, Singaporean, and American, given my unique international background, as cliche as it sounds, I identify as a "Third Culture Kid" more than anything else.

I am currently a senior at New York University studying Computer Science and Data Science in the College of Arts and Sciences. Upon graduation, I plan to work as a Data Scientist at Ernst & Young in the Tax, Technology, and Transformation department. I also currently work as a Business Intelligence Intern at Brooklyn Sports & Entertainment Global, a grader for the Computer Science Department at NYU, and IT Support at the Gallatin School of Individualized Study at NYU. While I'm unsure what I want to do long-term, I'm most interested in Software Engineering, Data Science, and Product Management.

I have a wide range of things I like to do for fun! My main hobbies are dancing (specifically hip-hop and contemporary), video editing, baking, and going to fitness classes (love spin and boxing!). I also love to travel, eat out, sing karaoke, and generally try whatever's new and exciting that's going on around me :)
